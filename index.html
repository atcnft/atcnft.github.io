@CNFT is a revolutionary Cardano NFT protocol that adds utility to any nft collection by providing a way to identify a NFT holding wallet address through combing the collection and NFT names with "@" and "#" signs.

Example:
“Awsome ABCD” collection registered their policy as “ABCD”, the following can be used as addresses to the wallet that holds “Awsome ABCD #999999999” NFT:

    ABCD#999999999 OR 999999999@ABCD

Important message to any NFT owner:
Always verify the address that you will send it your assets!

What is the process to register an @CNFT to my collection?

The process simply is;
(1) Registration -> (2) Verification -> (3) Mint

Where;
(1) Registration Request: Collection/policy owner is expected to fill this form,

(2) Verification: We will conduct a simple verification process through the provided collection details and contact information. Essentially, and after verifiying the collection itself, we will verify that the founder/owner issued this request,

(3) Mint: We will provide a link to mint your @CNFT. Once the transaction is completed, you will receive your @CNFT as a NFT in the specified wallet.

* The expected time to complete the above process depends on the volume of applicants. We aim to conclude any application in 24H.

Are @CNFTs case sensitive?

Yes! ABCD is NOT same as abcd, to support both, they should be minted separately.

Is there any guideline when picking a @CNFT name for my collection?

Initially, it should be inspired and derived from the collection name with unique alphanumeric characters only. Whitespaces are valid.

Some samples (assuming the picked @CNFT is not already taken):

Collection Name @CNFT
Awsome ABCD Awsome ABCD Valid
Awsome ABCD ABCD Valid
Awsome ABCD AB!CD Invalid
Awsome ABCD AABCD Valid
Awsome ABCD XYZ Invalid
